# [somethingforyou.fyi](https://somethingforyou.fyi/)

A cute and simple [site](https://somethingforyou.fyi/) to reveal your feelings and ask out your crush! 

### Motivation
I mean, hey, why should programming always be so serious? You don't always have to build a complex SaaS/tool/integration to help people, so I decided to build this as a creative way to ask out the girl/guy of your dreams.
